Mask With Love

My mom has always loved to sew.

Growing up, she would sew us clothing like sweaters, socks, hats, and pajama bottoms. As a teenager, I never wanted to wear anything she made, because I wanted to wear something I could purchase, something that the cool kids at school were wearing. However, as I have grown into adulthood, I've come to appreciate the gift my mom has. My mom is the type of individual who can follow any sewing pattern and present you with a beautifully and sturdy made item. She also has the gift to look past patterns and sew pieces from memory, from intuition.

My mom is the kind of human who uses her skills to help other people. She would rather make you a gift than buy one. She cherishes homemade ones the most. Recently as the world found itself unwell and people social-distancing, my mom decided to protect people by making face masks. Utilizing the fabrics in her sewing room, she has made masks for the family and friends. As a way to raise awareness about my mom's masks that she made with love, I create this website so people could have one.
